### Overall Evaluation

The working environment is very friendly and open. FCJ members are always ready to support me whenever I face difficulties, even outside of working hours. The workspace is tidy and comfortable, which helps me focus better.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The Team Admin supported me by guiding problem-solving mindsets and creating favorable conditions for me to work effectively.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assigned tasks align well with my academic background while allowing me to access modern Cloud technologies that meet enterprise needs. As a result, I have acquired many practical skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork, and professional communication in a corporate environment. Mentors also shared practical experiences that helped me better orient my career path.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
There is mutual respect among everyone. The team maintains a professional attitude when necessary but also knows how to have fun to keep the atmosphere stress-free.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company facilitates interns participation in events to gain new knowledge for project completion, as well as for future application.
